Our Timeline

2012

A journey of digital transformation, ad-tech innovation, and market dominance. From our inception as a lean start-up to leading the connected TV and intent-led video advertising space.

Foundation and Inception

The Launch: Adomantra is founded by technology and advertising specialist Dr. Vikas Katoch in Delhi, India.

Early Core: Starting with a lean 3-member team, the company operates primarily as a cross-screen video and rich media ad network. The mission is to bridge the gap between creative marketing and data-driven performance metrics.

Official Incorporation

Corporate Structure: The company officially registers under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) as Adomantra Digital India Private Limited on October 8, 2014, establishing its corporate headquarters in New Delhi.

Expansion into Travel Tech (Adotrip)

Diversification: Adomantra capitalizes on its digital marketing infrastructure to launch Adotrip, a dynamic, one-stop online travel and ticketing platform.

Tech Play: Adotrip features an AI-driven circuit planner to curate personalized travel itineraries and includes a community-driven travel blog section called Trip Talkies.

Programmatic Scaling & New Platforms

Enhanced Ad Tech: Adomantra significantly upgrades its tech stack, deploying proprietary algorithms and AI-driven Demand-Side Platforms (DSP) to optimize real-time bidding, user intent matching, and audience segmentation.

Premium Ecosystem: The company introduces Adopremium to cater to specialized, premium publisher networks and high-impact ad formats.

Geographic Spread: The agency scales its operations by opening regional offices and setting up teams in major Indian business hubs, including Mumbai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad.

Pivot to Connected TV (CTV) & Shoppable Video

Format Evolution: Recognizing the shift in media consumption, Adomantra pivots heavily toward CTV (Connected TV) advertising and interactive formats like shoppable videos and AR/VR-integrated ads.

Milestone Achievements: By this phase, the agency surpasses a milestone of serving over 10 billion ad impressions globally and running more than 1,000 active digital campaigns across core sectors like Healthcare, Automobile, Real Estate, and IT.

Full-Stack Dominance & "Intent-Led" Marketing

Modern Positioning: Adomantra solidifies its identity as a full-stack digital powerhouse, heavily emphasizing "intent-led video advertising"—serving hyper-personalized ad journeys based on real-time user behavior rather than just passive demographics.

Financial Footprint: Operating entirely as an unfunded, bootstrapped enterprise, the agency reports an annual revenue of ₹8.81 crore for the fiscal year ending in March 2025, maintaining its foothold as a highly agile, independent player in the competitive Indian ad tech landscape.
