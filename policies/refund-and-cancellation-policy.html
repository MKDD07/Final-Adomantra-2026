REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY

Effective Date: 16 Jun 26

Last Updated: 16 Jun 26

1 OVERVIEW

Adomantra is committed to delivering high-quality digital marketing, technology, and consulting services to our clients. We invest significant time, expertise, resources, and strategic effort into every client engagement from the outset.

This Refund and Cancellation Policy ("Policy") sets out the terms and conditions under which clients may cancel services, request refunds, or raise billing disputes. Please read this Policy carefully before engaging our services. By engaging Adomantra for any service, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Policy.

2 SERVICE CANCELLATION TERMS

2.1 PROJECT-BASED SERVICES

Project-based services include, but are not limited to: website design and development, e-commerce development, branding, logo design, one-time SEO audits, creative campaigns, UI/UX design, and content creation projects.

BEFORE WORK COMMENCEMENT (within 48 hours of payment): If cancellation is requested within 48 hours of payment and before any work has commenced, Adomantra will refund 100% of the amount paid, less any bank processing fees or transaction charges incurred.

AFTER WORK COMMENCEMENT BUT BEFORE 25% PROJECT COMPLETION: If the project has commenced and up to 25% of the work has been completed (as reasonably assessed by Adomantra), the Client will be refunded 50% of the total project fee. The retained 50% covers discovery, planning, research, and initial design/development work completed.

AFTER 25% PROJECT COMPLETION: No refund shall be issued once 25% or more of the project work has been completed. All fees paid shall be retained by Adomantra to cover work performed. Any remaining unpaid milestones shall be released from obligation with mutual written consent.

UPON FINAL DELIVERY: No refund is available once final deliverables have been submitted to the Client for review or approval, regardless of whether formal sign-off has been provided.

2.2 MONTHLY RETAINER SERVICES

Monthly retainer services include ongoing SEO management, social media management, content marketing, email marketing, performance marketing management, analytics and reporting, and similar continuous service packages.

NOTICE PERIOD: Either party may terminate a monthly retainer by providing a minimum of 30 (thirty) calendar days' written notice prior to the next billing cycle. Notice must be submitted in writing to info@adomantra.com.

FEES DURING NOTICE PERIOD: The Client remains obligated to pay the full monthly retainer fee for the duration of the 30-day notice period. Services will continue to be delivered during this period.

NO PRO-RATA REFUNDS: Monthly retainer fees are invoiced and committed on a monthly basis. No pro-rata refunds will be issued for partial months of service, mid-month cancellations, or underutilised service hours.

MINIMUM CONTRACT PERIOD: Where a minimum contract period has been agreed in writing (e.g., a 3-month or 6-month minimum commitment), the Client is obligated to fulfil that minimum term. Early termination before the minimum period expires shall incur an early termination fee equal to the remaining monthly fees owed for the minimum contract period.

2.3 MARKETING CAMPAIGN SERVICES

Marketing campaign services include managed paid advertising campaigns on Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and other platforms.

MANAGEMENT FEES: Adomantra's campaign management fee for any given month is non-refundable once that billing period has commenced, as it covers strategic planning, account management, and campaign execution already performed or in progress.

ADVERTISING SPEND: Any advertising spend placed with third-party platforms (Google, Meta, LinkedIn, etc.) is non-refundable by Adomantra. Unused advertising budget may be subject to partial refund directly from the relevant platform, governed by that platform's refund policy. Adomantra will assist clients in pursuing such refunds but cannot guarantee their outcome.

CAMPAIGN PAUSE: As an alternative to cancellation, Clients may request a campaign pause for up to 30 days without incurring additional fees. Management fees during a pause period will not be charged. Extensions beyond 30 days may incur a retainer fee to maintain account management.

2.4 WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT SERVICES

DISCOVERY AND STRATEGY PHASE: Fees paid for initial discovery, strategy sessions, wireframing, and technical scoping are non-refundable upon delivery of the discovery output, regardless of whether the Client proceeds to development.

DESIGN PHASE: Design fees are non-refundable once design concepts have been presented to the Client, whether or not the Client has formally approved them.

DEVELOPMENT PHASE: Development fees are non-refundable for completed development milestones.

HOSTING AND DOMAIN FEES: Any third-party hosting, domain registration, or SSL certificate fees paid through Adomantra are non-refundable as these are billed directly by third-party providers.

3 REFUND ELIGIBILITY

3.1 ELIGIBLE REFUND CIRCUMSTANCES

Adomantra will consider a refund request in the following circumstances:

  1. DUPLICATE PAYMENT: If the Client has been charged twice for the same invoice due to a system or processing error, the duplicate amount will be refunded in full.
  1. OVERCHARGE: If an invoice has been issued for an amount exceeding what was agreed in the signed Project Document or MSA, the overcharged amount will be refunded after verification.
  1. SERVICE NOT COMMENCED: If the Client has paid for a service that Adomantra has not yet commenced (outside the 48-hour cancellation window for project-based services), a refund may be considered at Adomantra's discretion, with applicable deductions for onboarding and planning work.
  1. ADOMANTRA FAILURE TO DELIVER: If Adomantra materially fails to deliver agreed services due to our own default (not attributable to Client delays or third-party factors), a partial or full refund of the affected service fee may be offered at Adomantra's discretion, after a good-faith review.

3.2 NON-REFUNDABLE FEES

The following fees are expressly non-refundable in all circumstances:

  1. Onboarding, discovery, and strategy fees, once the relevant sessions or outputs have been delivered
  2. Monthly management/retainer fees for any month in which services have been initiated
  3. Advertising spend placed with third-party platforms
  4. Third-party tool, software, or subscription fees paid on the Client's behalf
  5. Domain registration, hosting, and SSL certificate fees
  6. Rush or expedited service surcharges
  7. Bank transfer fees, international wire transfer charges, and payment processing fees
  8. Consultation fees for paid advisory sessions, once delivered

3.3 PARTIAL REFUND CONDITIONS

Partial refunds may be considered at Adomantra's sole discretion in the following circumstances:

  1. Project cancellation where work has commenced but is less than 25% complete (as described in Section 2.1)
  2. Service dissatisfaction documented in writing and reviewed through our formal dispute resolution process
  3. Force majeure events that permanently prevent Adomantra from delivering a contracted service

All partial refund requests must be submitted in writing to info@adomantra.com within 14 days of the triggering event. Requests submitted beyond this window may not be considered.

4. CLIENT-INITIATED TERMINATION

4.1 If the Client terminates a service engagement for any reason other than Adomantra's material breach:

  1. All fees paid to date for work completed or in progress are non-refundable
  2. All outstanding invoices for work performed and expenses incurred up to the termination date become immediately due and payable
  3. Any agreed minimum contract period fees remain payable as per the signed agreement
  4. The Client is responsible for the costs of transitioning campaigns, accounts, and assets to another provider or in-house team

4.2 ACCOUNT AND DATA TRANSFER

Upon termination and receipt of all outstanding payments, Adomantra will:

  • Transfer advertising account access back to the Client within 5 business days
  • Provide the Client with all campaign performance data, reports, and creative assets produced during the engagement
  • Remove Adomantra team access from Client platforms

5. ADOMANTRA-INITIATED TERMINATION

Adomantra reserves the right to terminate a service engagement under the following circumstances:

  1. NON-PAYMENT: Non-payment of any invoice within 30 days of the due date despite written notice
  2. BREACH OF TERMS: Material breach of our Terms & Conditions or Master Service Agreement by the Client
  3. PLATFORM POLICY VIOLATIONS: If the Client's products, services, or requested activities violate the policies of advertising platforms, exposing Adomantra to policy violations or account risks
  4. LEGAL OR ETHICAL CONCERNS: If continued engagement would expose Adomantra to legal liability or require us to engage in activities contrary to our ethical standards

In the event of Adomantra-initiated termination for any of the above reasons, no refund shall be issued for fees already paid, and all outstanding invoices shall remain due and payable.

In the event of Adomantra-initiated termination not attributable to Client default, a pro-rata refund for undelivered services will be provided.

6 BILLING DISPUTES

6.1 DISPUTE PROCESS

If you believe an invoice contains an error or that you have been charged incorrectly, you must notify Adomantra in writing at info@adomantra.com within 7 (seven) business days of the invoice date, specifying:

  1. The invoice number and amount in dispute
  2. The reason for the dispute with supporting documentation
  3. The amount you believe is correctly owed

6.2 UNDISPUTED AMOUNTS

The undisputed portion of any invoice must be paid by the original due date, regardless of any dispute relating to other items on the same invoice.

6.3 RESOLUTION TIMELINE

Adomantra will acknowledge your dispute within 2 business days and aim to resolve it within 15 business days. Where additional time is required, we will inform you of the expected resolution timeline.

7 CHARGEBACK POLICY

Filing a chargeback with your bank or payment card provider without first attempting to resolve the dispute directly with Adomantra is a violation of this Policy and our service agreement.

If a chargeback is filed:

  1. Adomantra reserves the right to immediately suspend all active services
  2. We will contest the chargeback and provide all relevant documentation to the payment processor
  3. The Client may be liable for chargeback fees imposed on Adomantra by the payment processor
  4. Adomantra reserves the right to pursue legal action to recover amounts owed

We encourage all billing concerns to be resolved through our disputes process before escalating to a financial institution.

8 REFUND PROCESSING TIMELINE

Where a refund is approved by Adomantra:

  1. Refunds will be processed within 10–15 business days of the written approval confirmation
  2. Refunds will be returned via the same payment method used for the original transaction, wherever possible
  3. International wire transfer refunds may take an additional 3–7 business days due to banking processing times
  4. Adomantra is not responsible for delays caused by the Client's bank or payment processor once the refund has been initiated on our end
  5. Any bank transfer fees, currency conversion charges, or international processing fees associated with the refund will be deducted from the refund amount
